Example 13 - A Scaled Pre-Roll Linear Interactive Ad that maintains the Aspect Ratio

This example illustrates how a scaled linear interactive ad that does maintain the aspect ratio will appear in the player. To achieve this, the VAST template is returning "maintainAspectRatio=true scalable=true" within the MediaFile tag. These settings result in the Flowplayer clip.scaling property being set to 'fit'.
