1. Introduction

OVA supports VAST compliant linear and non-linear ads. Both ad types can have accompanying companions. Non-linear ads can be placed as either overlays or non-overlays. VPAID ads are also supported in linear and non-linear overlay formats.

2. Linear Ads

Linear ads take the form of pre, mid or post-roll ads that can be either non-interactive ad streams (e.g. mp4, flv etc. formats), or interactive (VPAID SWF) ads.

2.1 Non-Interactive Linear Ads

Non-Interactive linear ads are "progressive" or "streaming" ad clips that may have an optional "click-through" specified. If a "click-through" is specified, a "click me" style ad notice will appear as the viewer mouses over the player during playback.

For further information regarding OVA and Linear ads, please consult the Delivering Linear Ads guide.

2.2 Interactive Linear Ads

Interactive Linear ads are VPAID pre, mid or post-roll ads. VPAID ads are IAB compliant interactive ad types that support user interaction with the ad as it plays back.

Click on the image to view a linear VPAID ad example.

For further information regarding OVA and VPAID ads, please consult the VPAID and Linear Ads guide.

3. Non-Linear Ads

Non-Linear ads are either placed on top of the player as "overlays" or placed around the player as "non-overlays".

3.1 Non-Linear Overlay Ads

OVA supports non-linear overlay ads in various creative formats:

  • Image
  • HTML
  • Text
  • Non-VPAID SWF
  • VPAID
  • iFrame
  • JavaScript

4. Companion Ads

Companion ads accompany linear and non-linear ad types and are displayed within DIV regions around the player. Companion ads can be of various formats from images to Flash SWF and script based creatives.
